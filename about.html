About SmartGrocery

Reimagining grocery shopping with intelligence

SmartGrocery is a modern grocery e-commerce platform that combines the convenience of online shopping with AI-powered recommendations. Our smart recommendation engine analyzes your family size and weekly budget to suggest the perfect grocery list — saving you time, money, and effort.

Built as a full-stack project using HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, PHP, and XML, this platform demonstrates how technology can transform everyday tasks into seamless experiences.
